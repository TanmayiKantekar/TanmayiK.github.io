Who I Am

About

"I approach data the way I approach books — I don't just scan for the answer. I want to understand the argument."

Senior data scientist with a dual background in Computer Science and Finance. That combination isn't incidental — it means I'm as comfortable in a model review as I am in a risk committee.

I've led the full ML lifecycle: feature engineering, deployment via Airflow-orchestrated pipelines, MLflow experiment tracking, and PSI-based drift monitoring. I build systems designed to hold up in production.

CFA Level 1 cleared. MBA in Finance. I can write a SHAP waterfall explanation a CFO will understand and a model card a regulator won't redline.

Outside work, I read obsessively. Mostly non-fiction — Kahneman, Nate Silver, Hofstadter. The best data scientists share something with good readers: comfort with uncertainty and willingness to update on evidence.
